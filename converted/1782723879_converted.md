CPU:

Storage:

    256 GB SSD (their own storage infrastructure)

How the Streaming Works

Your Laptop  ←──── video stream (H.264/H.265) ────  Shadow Server
     │                                                      │
 Just shows                                          Runs everything
 the picture                                         (game, OS, GPU)
 + sends your
 keyboard/mouse

Your laptop is basically just a thin client — it decodes video and sends input back. Even a potato laptop works as long as your internet is good (~15 Mbps minimum, 50+ Mbps ideal).
The Tech Stack

    Streaming protocol: Their own proprietary low-latency protocol (not standard RDP or VNC)
    OS on server: Full Windows 10/11
    Virtualization: Each user gets an isolated virtual machine with dedicated GPU passthrough — not shared GPU, which is the key difference from cheap cloud VMs
